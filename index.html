ABOUT ME

Inquisitive, creative computer science engineer with a strong foundation in software and DevSecOps engineering and programming principles. My journey in software development began with a fascination for solving real-world problems through code, and it has since evolved into a career where I thrive on tackling challenges and continuously learning.

When Hansom Robotics unveiled SOPHIA, A humanoid Robot in 2016, the entire world stood up to take notice of this momentous feat of Engineering. As a 14-year-old, I was baffled by the sheer ingenuity involved in building a humanoid robot that was capable of duplicating human dexterity. While people were still talking about the ‘what’ of it, I was intrigued towards ‘how’ of it.
Thus, I dived head first into this multidisciplinary field of Computer Science and Artificial Intelligence with a desire to one day contribute to its biggest achievements.

Beyond coding, I am driven by the impact my work can have on users and businesses. Whether it’s improving user experiences, optimizing processes, or contributing to a larger mission, I believe in creating meaningful outcomes through technology.

Let’s connect! Whether you're looking to discuss technology trends, explore potential collaborations, or simply share insights, feel free to reach out. I look forward to connecting with you!
